## History

### The Ancient Era
Before recorded history, a powerful civilization known as the Arcadians ruled these islands. They possessed advanced magic and technology, creating wonders that still dot the landscape today.

### The Fall
Some cataclysmic event led to the downfall of the Arcadian civilization. Ruins and artifacts are all that remain.

### The Modern Era
In recent centuries, explorers from the mainland have begun to rediscover the archipelago, establishing settlements and uncovering ancient secrets.
