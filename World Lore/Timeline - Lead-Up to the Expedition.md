# Timeline - Lead-Up to the Expedition

Player-facing events leading up to the flotilla's departure.  
See also [[World Lore/Campaign Overview]] and [[World Lore/The Opening of the Archipelago]].

The formal international flotilla coalition is only six months old at launch; earlier entries track the pre-flotilla buildup that led to its formation.

> [!info] Arcadian Year Conversion
> For this campaign timeline, use: **AR = Earth Year + 2700**.
>
> - Example: **2026 CE** corresponds to **4726 AR**.
> - Timeline anchor: **Expedition Start = 4726 AR, Jan 12**.

## The Lead-Up to the Expedition

- **Expedition T-6 years:** The first known Arcadian run associated with [[The Red Wake]] departs under Captain Blackblood, with [[NPCs/Flotilla/The Red Wake/Captain Serafina Marlowe|Serafina Marlowe]] serving as first mate.
- **Expedition T-4 years:** A second Red Wake expedition leaves with Blackblood still in command; dockside accounts place Marlowe as his first mate again.
- **Expedition T-3 years, 2 months:** The second voyage returns under Marlowe's command after Blackblood's reported death at sea, and many sailors treat this as the moment she inherited both ship and crew.
- **Expedition T-3 years:** Rare Arcadian artifacts begin appearing in high-end salerooms through unclear brokers, fueling rumors of a shadow trade route into the islands.
- **Expedition T-2 years:** Scholars linked to [[Factions/The Magaambya|The Magaambya]] publish cautious notes suggesting the Archipelago's magical barriers may be shifting and call for structured field research.
- **Expedition T-21 months:** Merchant houses and private patrons fund a limited test venture of scholars, pilots, and surveyors to verify whether safe passages can be mapped consistently.
- **Expedition T-20 months:** Early internal briefings begin around [[Factions/The Chelish Expeditionary Authority|the Chelish Expeditionary Authority]], with delegates and observers reacting to Chelish public statements.
- **Expedition T-19 months:** In response to Chelish involvement, [[Factions/The Gray Corsairs|the Gray Corsairs]] publicly enter the dispute, marking the start of a bureaucratic process that drags on for more than a year and later becomes a core part of flotilla governance.
- **Expedition T-18 months:** Follow-up reports from those test routes are treated as confirmation that barrier behavior is changing in measurable, if still dangerous, patterns.
- **Expedition T-14 months:** News spreads that an Aspis Consortium expedition has gone missing; investor losses and broken contracts ripple through multiple ports.
- **Expedition T-8 months:** Pre-charter negotiations harden into legal standoffs as Chelish procedural objections collide with Gray Corsair anti-imperial demands, forcing repeated mediation by Assembly diplomats.
- **Expedition T-6 months:** The Hands' plague-vision warnings become the wake-up moment.
- **Expedition T-5 months:** Representatives later associated with [[The Assembly of Accord]] begin formal charter debates on command structure and legal authority.
- **Expedition T-5 months:** The Hands facing limited funding, they shift from private charter attempts to public appeals and negotiations with governments, and major powers formally constitute the flotilla coalition.
- **Expedition T-4 months:** Major factions accept the core structure of the Charter of Shared Waters and the Common Purse, trading speed for a legally binding coalition framework.
- **Expedition T-3 months:** Supply contracts accelerate, and crews linked to [[Factions/The New Army of Exploration|The New Army of Exploration]] are seen drilling in harbor.
- **Expedition T-6 weeks:** Priests and medics from [[Factions/The Hands of the Everlight|The Hands of the Everlight]] continue public pressure to sail without further delay.
- **Expedition T+0 (4726 AR, Jan 12):** A multinational flotilla finally departs under shared but visibly tense leadership.
- **Expedition T+2 months:** The flotilla arrives at the edge of the Ironbound Archipelago for final preparations before entering the Arcadian seas, waiting in safe waters for [[The Red Wake]] to declare weather conditions favorable for the first crossing.
